Natural Language Processing is a field of artificial intelligence.
NLP helps machines understand human language.
Tokenization is the process of splitting text into words.
Stop word removal helps reduce noise in text data.
Stemming converts words into their root form.
Lemmatization is better than stemming in many cases.
TF IDF is used to find important words in documents.
Bag of Words represents text using word counts.
Cosine similarity measures how similar two documents are.
Chatbots use NLP techniques to respond to users.
Machine learning is widely used in NLP applications.
Deep learning models perform well in language tasks.
Python is commonly used for NLP development.
Regular expressions help clean text data.
Text preprocessing improves model performance.
Search engines use TF IDF based ranking.
Information retrieval is an important NLP application.
AI systems depend on quality training data.
Vector space models convert text into numbers.
Similarity matching is used in question answering systems.
